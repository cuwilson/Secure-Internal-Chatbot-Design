Muffin Maniacs – Payroll FAQ
1. When Do I Get Paid?
• Employees are paid biweekly on Fridays.
2. How Do I Access My Paystubs?
• Paystubs are available online through the Employee Portal.
3. Overtime Rules
• Non-exempt employees receive 1.5x pay for hours worked over 40 per week.
4. Direct Deposit
• Changes to bank information must be submitted at least 7 days before payday.
5. Payroll Issues
• Questions or discrepancies should be reported to HR within 5 business days.

Muffin Maniacs – PTO (Paid Time Off) Policy
1. Overview
Paid Time Off (PTO) provides eligible employees with paid time away from work for vacation, personal needs, and planned absences.
2. Eligibility
• Full-time employees: eligible after 30 days of employment.
• Part-time employees: prorated PTO after 60 days.
3. Accrual Rates
• Full-time: 1 day per month (12 days per year).
• Part-time: prorated based on hours worked.
4. Request Procedure
• Requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.
• Approval is subject to staffing needs.
5. Carryover
• Employees may carry over up to 5 unused PTO days per year.
6. Payout
• Unused PTO is paid out upon termination if the employee provides proper notice.

Muffin Maniacs – Attendance & Conduct Policy

1. Attendance Expectations
Employees are expected to:
• Arrive on time for scheduled shifts. Arrival after 10 min of the start of the shift will be logged as a tardy
• Report absences at least 2 hours before shift start.
• Maintain consistent attendance.

2. Tardiness
• Three tardies in a 30-day period result in a written warning.

3. No-Call/No-Show
• One no-call/no-show may result in disciplinary action.
• Two may result in termination.

4. Employee Conduct
All employees must:
• Treat coworkers respectfully.
• Follow safety and sanitation rules.
• Represent the company positively when speaking with customers.

5. Disciplinary Steps
• Verbal warning
• Written warning
• Final warning
• Termination
